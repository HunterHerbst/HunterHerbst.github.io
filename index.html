Introduction

The purpose of this website is to guide you through the process of using the live streaming platform Twitch. Additionally, this site will teach you how to grow the community you are creating through the use of other social media platforms.

Getting Started

Downloading Software

There are two pieces of software you will need to install:

  • Open Broadcaster Software (OBS) Download here
  • Discord Download here
OBS is the software you will be using to record our screen or camera input, and stream to Twitch. Discord is a social media platform that is commonly used by streamers to communicate with their community members. You will be using Discord to create a hub for conversation between members as well as telling people in the community when you go live.

Creating Accounts

You will need to create accounts for the following services in order to use them:

  • Google/Gmail
  • Twitch
  • Discord
  • Twitter (Optional)
The reason for creating a new Gmail/Google account is because you will probably want to keep the public and private sides of your life separate. Streaming and connecting a Google account to many services related to streaming will bring spam to your email, so overall it is better to separate a streaming email from your personal email. Twitter is optional and will be used to send out a tweet to followers for when you go live.
